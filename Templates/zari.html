When I started high school, I had no idea what I wanted to study in college. I knew that
I wanted to do something related to either math or computer, but I didn't know exactly
what. I took a bunch of classes while in high school in things like Photoshop and CGI.
After a family member suggested I look into computer science, I took an Intro to Coding class
towards the end of my junior year and knew that I wanted to study Computer Science ever since.

To high school students: Try new things until you find out what you're interested in. It's okay
to go into college unsure of your major. It's also okay to change your mind and switch majors if you
realize you are interested in something else. Start thinking about college applications as soon as possible.
Start thinking about college applications as soon as possible. Do as much research as you can to
make sure you don't end up in a school or major you don't like.
