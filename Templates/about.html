ABOUT

Surviving the University Experience is a web application that was developled to help first-time students
navigate the process and experience of college/university. Throughout the site, you will find many resources
to help with the college process.You can calculate your college cost by using the financial aid calculator,
you can use the Khan Academy and Youtube pages to gethelp on particular topics, and you may explore the
resources page for scholarship information, tips and other pointers on surviving away from home.
